Whiskey Sour

One of the worlds oldest cocktails first dated to 1862.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces bourbon
  • 3/4 ounce lemon juice
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • 1/2 ounce egg white
  • Angostura bitters

Steps

  1. Add bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a shaker and dry-shake for 30 seconds without ice
  2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled
  3. Strain into a rocks glass or coupe
  4. Garnish with 3 or 4 drops of Angostura bitters
